Puppeteer Your Robot: Augmented Reality Leader-Follower Teleoperation

Jonne van Haastregt*, Michael C. Welle*, Yuchong Zhang, Danica Kragic

Obtaining high-quality demonstration is key for many state-of-the-art methods that tackle complex and challenging manipulation tasks. In this work, we introduce a way to puppeteer a robot by controlling a virtual robot in an augmented reality setting. This system allows us to retain the advantages of being very intuitive from a physical Leader-follower approach while cutting the hardware cost in half from a completely physical setup and endowing the user with additional information using Augmented reality. We validate our system with a pilot user study $n=10$ on a block stacking and rice scooping task where the overwhelming majority rates the system favorably.
